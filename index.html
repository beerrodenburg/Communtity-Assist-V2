Our Mission

Building bridges between people who want to help and kids who need it.

Community Assist is a collective of Indonesian businesses — founded by Indosole and PNNY — raising funds to empower local communities through education, environment and opportunity.

Everything we raise goes to our partners' scholarship programs, helping children stay in school and build a future for themselves and their families.

  • Indosole
    Founding partner

    Recycled-footwear pioneer, born in Bali.

  • PNNY
    Founding partner

    Bali's home-grown music collective, est. 2014.

  • Ragam Foundation
    Scholarship partner

    "Ragam" means diversity — runs the scholarship program.

  • Green School
    Scholarship partner

    Bali's landmark sustainability school.

Ni Putu Chintya Pratiwi just graduated in accounting from Universitas Mahasaraswati Denpasar and landed her first job — while her mum opened a laundry business. One scholarship, a whole family's future.

Top of her class

Keeps top grades at SMA Saraswati Denpasar, leads student council and theatre, and tutors kids to help her parents. She wants to be a tax officer.

Back in the classroom

Where your money goes

Rp 6 million sends one child to school for a whole year.

  • Quality education

    A full year of tuition for one student.

  • Learning materials

    Books, pens, a backpack — the essentials.

  • School uniform

    A complete uniform so they fit in and show up.

  • A dedicated mentor

    A community manager guiding each child's progress.
